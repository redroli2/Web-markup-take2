Care Instructions:

  1. There is enough food available for each cat.
  2. Please feed the cat once in the morning and once in the evening. You will find pre-packaged cans in the apartment. One can corresponds to one meal.
  3. Please also give the cat enough to drink.
  4. There are cat flaps in the apartment. The cat goes out and back in on its own. Please do not pressure it to do anything.
  5. The cats know their way around the city well. Do not worry if they are outside sometimes. They come back to the apartment quickly and happily.
  6. There is also a litter box in the apartment, which needs to be emptied every 2 days. You will find the necessary utensils for that in the apartment. The business then goes into an extra trash can next to the litter box.
  7. Please show the cat that you care about it. Please do not abuse the cat or cause it any harm. The cats are trained to give us owners a sign if something like that happens should.
  8. Cuddling sessions with the cat are also welcome, because they like that the most:)

That's it. Don't be put off by the list. It looks more than it is. The most important thing is that you have fun with the whole thing and have a good time with the cat in a beautiful city! See you soon:)
